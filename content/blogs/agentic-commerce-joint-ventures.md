In April 2026, we started pursuing joint ventures with payment companies to help merchants get ready for agentic commerce.

Our view is that agentic commerce will not be won by chat UX alone. It depends on whether catalog quality, trust signals, and transaction rails can operate together under clear constraints. Payment partners are central to that operating model because they already sit at the intersection of merchant risk, compliance, and conversion.

## What we are solving for merchants

- Better product understanding before checkout decisions.
- Stronger trust and verification signals in agent-assisted buying flows.
- More consistent operations from feed ingestion through downstream channel syndication.

Our current ecommerce offering at Paladio highlights this direction, including production catalog agent deployments and operational metrics around enrichment speed and error reduction: [Paladio ecommerce agents](https://www.paladio.ai/agents/ecommerce).

## Why joint ventures now

If we want merchants to adopt agentic workflows safely, infrastructure and incentives need to align. Working jointly with payment ecosystem players helps move from isolated AI features to production systems that merchants can actually rely on.
