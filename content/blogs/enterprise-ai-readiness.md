Most enterprises fail AI not because models are weak, but because **ownership is fuzzy** and **data is not a product**. Durable programs centralize a small set of high-value workflows, define who signs off on training data and releases, and measure the same KPIs in shadow and live modes. Without that spine, every new use case rebuilds the same plumbing—and fatigue sets in.
